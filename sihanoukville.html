Trip Overview

Securing Funding for Taskly's Growth

"A critical two-day trip to Sihanoukville focused on presenting Taskly's strategic expansion plans and securing vital investment for our upcoming digital transformation initiatives, particularly those focused on enhancing Taskly's AI capabilities and seamless integration features."

This was a highly focused, two-day business trip to Sihanoukville, Cambodia, undertaken by a core six-member Taskly team. Operating on a lean budget, our main goal was to engage directly with two potential investors – Coastal Horizons Group and Galaxy Capital Ventures. We aimed to present Taskly's ambitious expansion plans and compelling investment opportunities, hoping to secure strategic funding vital for our upcoming digital transformation initiatives, particularly those focused on enhancing Taskly's AI capabilities and seamless integration features. The meetings were designed for efficient discussions, clear articulation of our value proposition, and building foundational relationships while being extremely cost-conscious.
