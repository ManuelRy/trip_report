Business Trip Report

Sihanoukville Investor Meetings

Company

Mohanokor Corp.

Trip Dates

June 16 – June 17, 2025

Team Members

Mr. Sopheak Dara (CEO), Ms. Lina Kim (CFO)

1. Overview

"A two-day strategic trip to Sihanoukville to present our expansion plans and secure pivotal funding for our digital transformation initiatives."

This trip was designed to foster in-depth discussions with two potential investors, providing a comprehensive understanding of our company's robust value proposition and compelling investment opportunities.

2. Trip Highlights

A chronological report of all key activities.

Travel & Hotel Check-in

The team departed from Phnom Penh at 8:00 AM, arriving at Sihanoukville International Airport at 8:45 AM. After a smooth transfer, check-in at the Independence Hotel was completed by 9:30 AM, followed by a final briefing for the first meeting.

Meeting: Coastal Horizons Group

A productive discussion on market growth and competitive advantages. Mr. Pham expressed significant interest in our sustainability framework.

Meeting: Galaxy Capital Ventures

After a working lunch, the team traveled to The Prince Times Hotel. The presentation emphasized our technological scalability, with Ms. Chen particularly keen on our AI-driven analytics platform. This was followed by networking and a team debrief.

Day 2: Strategy & Departure

The morning was dedicated to an internal strategy session to outline follow-up actions. After lunch at a local eatery, the team checked out and departed from Sihanoukville at 3:00 PM, arriving in Phnom Penh at 3:45 PM.

4. Conclusion

The Sihanoukville trip was highly productive and successfully met its primary objective. Both meetings yielded positive outcomes, with strong indications of moving to due diligence. The team effectively communicated our vision and presented a compelling investment case. We are optimistic about securing the necessary funding to fuel our strategic growth initiatives.
