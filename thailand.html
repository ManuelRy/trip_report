Business Trip Report

Thailand FutureAI Conference

Company

Taskly Software

Trip Dates

September 22 – September 24, 2025

Team Members

Headangelly Huy (Visionary Leader), Sokleng Ry (Technical Lead)

1. Overview

"A strategic three-day immersion into the future of AI-powered workplace solutions, positioning Taskly at the forefront of productivity innovation."

This comprehensive business trip to Bangkok was designed to gain cutting-edge knowledge on AI integration, particularly in intelligent scheduling and task prioritization. Our objectives included networking with global AI leaders, identifying potential AI tools for Taskly's platform, and exploring new innovation avenues to revolutionize productivity.

2. Trip Highlights

A comprehensive timeline of our AI conference experience.

Day 1: Arrival & Opening Keynote

The team departed from Phnom Penh at 7:00 AM, arriving at Suvarnabhumi Airport at 8:15 AM. After check-in at Amari Watergate Bangkok, we attended the opening keynote "AI as the New Colleague: Redefining Human-Machine Collaboration" by Dr. Li Wei, followed by workshops on predictive analytics for resource optimization.

Day 2: AI & Cybersecurity

Day two focused on AI in cybersecurity and project management optimization. The plenary session on enterprise AI protection was crucial for Taskly's platform security. We also had strategic vendor meetings with AI solution providers to explore integration opportunities.

Day 3: Closing Keynote & Strategic Insights

The final day featured the closing keynote "Beyond Automation: AI for Strategic Decision Making and Innovation" which perfectly aligned with Taskly's vision. We concluded with final networking sessions and collected valuable conference materials before departing Bangkok with actionable insights for our platform's AI integration roadmap.

4. Conclusion

Attending the "FutureAI Workplace Summit 2025" in Bangkok was an extremely valuable and insightful experience for our team. The conference provided comprehensive understanding of the latest trends, ethical considerations, and practical applications of AI in diverse workplace settings. The knowledge gained from keynotes, workshops, and direct interactions with AI solution providers will directly inform Taskly's strategy for integrating advanced AI tools to boost efficiency, enhance our scheduling algorithms, and drive innovation across our platform. We are now well-equipped to propose specific AI initiatives and pilot projects based on the intelligence gathered, further solidifying Taskly's commitment to revolutionizing personal and professional productivity.
