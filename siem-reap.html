Trip Overview

Exploring Siem Reap for Expansion

This five-day business trip to Siem Reap, Cambodia, was undertaken by Headangelly Huy and Sok Eng Huot to explore potential sites for Taskly’s expansion. The goal was to evaluate five strategic areas for their suitability to host a future operational hub, which could serve as a regional development center, customer support office, or creative innovation lab.

Key Assessment Criteria:

  • Infrastructure and accessibility
  • Availability of a local tech and service talent pool
  • Regulatory environment
  • Long-term growth and sustainability potential

The team also took time to engage with local culture, recognizing the value of cultural integration in successful expansion.

Detailed Itinerary

Trip Highlights

A chronological report of key activities and site visits in Siem Reap.

Morning-Afternoon: Travel & Arrival

8:00 AM: Departed from Larryta Phnom Penh.
2:50 PM: Arrived at Larryta Siem Reap Branch.
3:00 PM: Checked in at Sofitel Angkor Phokeethra Golf & Spa Resort.

Evening: Strategy Dinner

6:30 PM – 8:00 PM: Team dinner at The Local Khmer Restaurant to discuss initial impressions and strategic implications for the upcoming site visits.

Afternoon: Site Visit 1 & Debrief

12:00 PM – 1:00 PM: Lunch near Angkor Wat.
1:00 PM – 2:30 PM: Site Visit at Siem Reap-Angkor International Airport Area. Met with airport authority to assess infrastructure.
2:30 PM – 3:00 PM: Coffee break.
3:00 PM – 5:00 PM: Hotel debrief, data entry, and preliminary evaluation.

Morning: Site Visit 2

9:00 AM – 11:30 AM: Toured the Airport Road Corridor, meeting with a local land broker to evaluate plots suitable for data centers, R&D, and logistics.

Afternoon: Site Visit 3

12:30 PM – 4:00 PM: Explored the Wat Bo Area on the east bank of the Siem Reap River, focusing on commercial/boutique space and co-working potential. Conducted informal interviews with local business owners.

Evening: Cultural Immersion

6:00 PM – 7:30 PM: Attended dinner and a cultural show at Apsara Theatre for team bonding and cultural immersion.

Morning: Site Visit 4

9:00 AM – 12:00 PM: Assessed the Pub Street & Old Market Area for brand presence, pop-up experiences, and foot traffic. Met with real estate agents.

Afternoon: Site Visit 5

1:00 PM – 5:00 PM: Visited the Sala Kamreuk Community Zone to evaluate residential growth, workforce availability, and support center feasibility. Engaged with the commune council and residents.

Evening: Strategic Reflection

7:00 PM – 8:30 PM: Team dinner focused on strategic reflection of all five sites visited.

Full Day: Stakeholder Engagement & Data Analysis

Morning: Met with Ms. Chanthy Soeun, Director of the Siem Reap Provincial Department of Commerce, to discuss business registration, tech incentives, and investment policies.
Afternoon: Met with Paññāsāstra University of Cambodia (Siem Reap Campus) to discuss the talent pipeline, internships, and skill availability.
The remainder of the day was dedicated to synthesizing data, drafting a preliminary report, and considering financial implications.

Evening: Final Review

6:30 PM – 8:00 PM: Final team dinner and comprehensive review of the preliminary trip report.

Conclusion

The Siem Reap business trip was a major success in identifying viable expansion sites for Taskly.
