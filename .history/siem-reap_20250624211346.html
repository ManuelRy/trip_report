Siem Reap Expansion Research

Strategic location scouting for Taskly's next operational hub

July 14-18, 2025
Siem Reap, Cambodia
Visionary Leader & User Advocate
$1,924 Total Cost

Expansion Mission

This five-day intensive business trip to Siem Reap, Cambodia, was undertaken by Headangelly Huy (Visionary Leader) and Sok Eng Huot (User Advocate) to find potential locations for Taskly's expansion. The team's mission was to visit five specific areas to assess their suitability for our future operations.

Key Assessment Criteria

Infrastructure

Technology readiness & connectivity

Accessibility

Transportation & logistics

Talent Pool

Tech & customer service expertise

Regulatory Environment

Business-friendly policies

Growth Potential

Sustainable long-term development

Potential Operations: Regional development hub, customer support center, or creative innovation lab

Five Strategic Locations Assessed

Airport Development Zones

Location: Near Siem Reap-Angkor International Airport

Large land parcels for tech zones, excellent connectivity, future growth potential

Airport Road Corridor

Location: Main road to airport

Data centers, R&D facilities, good access and logistics potential

Wat Bo Commercial Zone

Location: East bank of Siem Reap River

Boutique offices, creative spaces, vibrant business environment

Pub Street & Old Market

Location: Central Siem Reap

High traffic, branding presence, client-facing opportunities

Sala Kamreuk Community

Location: South of city center

Cost-effective, community-oriented, residential proximity

Research Journey Highlights

Day 1 - Monday, July 14, 2025

8:00 AM
Departure to Siem Reap

Flight from Phnom Penh International Airport (PNH) to Siem Reap Angkor International Airport (SAI)

9:30 AM
Hotel Check-in & Team Briefing

Check-in at Borei Angkor Resort & Spa and initial team briefing on assessment criteria for potential Taskly locations

10:30 AM
Cultural Visit: Angkor Wat Temple Complex

Team bonding and appreciation of local heritage, relevant for understanding the region's appeal for attracting and retaining future Taskly talent

1:00 PM
Site Visit 1: Airport Development Zones

Location: Areas surrounding Siem Reap-Angkor International Airport, Sotr Nikum District

Assessment: Large land parcels for future commercial and industrial development, focusing on infrastructure readiness and airport connectivity advantages

Key Finding: High potential for large-scale Taskly office or data center with dedicated tech innovation zones

Day 2 - Tuesday, July 15, 2025

9:00 AM
Site Visit 2: Airport Road Corridor

Focus: Large land parcels suitable for data centers, specialized R&D facilities, or support centers requiring significant space and access

Met with local land broker specializing in commercial plots

12:30 PM
Site Visit 3: Wat Bo Area - Commercial & Boutique Zone

Location: East bank of Siem Reap River

Assessment: Existing commercial properties, boutique office spaces, and co-working potential

Appeal: Vibrant area for potential Taskly regional office or creative innovation lab with good appeal to tech talent

6:00 PM
Cultural Show and Dinner

Team building and cultural immersion at Apsara Theatre

Day 3 - Wednesday, July 16, 2025

9:00 AM
Site Visit 4: Pub Street & Old Market Area

Location: Central Siem Reap tourist and entertainment hub

Analysis: Commercial real estate for potential Taskly branding presence, pop-up experience centers, or client-facing office

Research: Property availability, foot traffic patterns, and market insights

1:00 PM
Site Visit 5: Sala Kamreuk Community Zone

Location: South of city center, rapidly developing residential and mixed-use area

Assessment: Local workforce availability, residential options for future staff, potential for community-oriented business models

Community Engagement: Spoke with commune council representatives and residents about talent pool and community dynamics

Day 4 - Thursday, July 17, 2025

8:30 AM
Stakeholder Meetings & Data Analysis

Morning Meeting: Ms. Chanthy Soeun (Director, Siem Reap Provincial Department of Commerce) - discussed business registration, investment incentives, and local economic policies for tech businesses

Afternoon Meeting: Representatives from Paññāsāstra University of Cambodia (Siem Reap Campus) - explored talent pipelines for software development and potential internship programs

Analysis Session: Comprehensive data analysis of all five sites, comparing pros and cons against Taskly's operational needs

Day 5 - Friday, July 18, 2025

9:00 AM
Final Strategic Discussion

Final internal discussion and agreement on top recommendations for expansion, outlining next steps for detailed feasibility studies and architectural planning

2:00 PM
Return to Phnom Penh

Flight back to Phnom Penh International Airport with comprehensive expansion research

Research Outcomes & Strategic Recommendations

The five-day trip to Siem Reap was an unqualified success in identifying and evaluating potential locations for Taskly's expansion. Headangelly Huy and Sok Eng Huot meticulously assessed five distinct areas, engaged with key local stakeholders, and gathered comprehensive data on infrastructure, talent, and growth potential.

Top Recommendations

Airport Development Zones

Highest potential for large-scale operations

Wat Bo Area

Ideal for creative innovation lab

The detailed site visits and stakeholder meetings, complemented by the cultural experience at Angkor Wat, provided invaluable insights that will inform our strategic decisions. We have identified several highly promising locations that align with Taskly's long-term objectives for a new operational hub or innovation lab.

Next Steps: A detailed internal report with specific recommendations and a comprehensive feasibility study will follow this trip report, enabling us to advance Taskly's mission of revolutionizing productivity.
