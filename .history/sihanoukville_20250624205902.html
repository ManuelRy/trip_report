Sihanoukville Investor Meetings

Strategic funding discussions for Taskly's expansion initiatives

June 16-17, 2025
Sihanoukville, Cambodia
CEO & CFO
$700 Total Cost

Trip Overview

This two-day business trip to Sihanoukville, Cambodia, was undertaken by our CEO, Mr. Sovandara Phallim, and CFO, Ms. Headangelly Huy, to engage with two different potential investors. The primary objective was to present our company's robust expansion plans and compelling investment opportunities to secure strategic funding for our upcoming digital transformation initiatives.

The meetings were designed to foster in-depth discussions and a comprehensive understanding of our value proposition, particularly focusing on our innovative approach to productivity solutions and market expansion strategies.

Conclusion

The two-day business trip to Sihanoukville was highly productive and successfully met its primary objective of engaging with potential investors. Both meetings, with Coastal Horizons Group and Galaxy Capital Ventures, yielded positive outcomes, with strong indications of moving forward to the due diligence phase.

The team effectively communicated our company's vision and presented compelling investment cases. We are optimistic about securing the necessary funding to fuel our strategic growth initiatives. Immediate next steps include preparing detailed follow-up documentation and scheduling virtual meetings to advance the discussions.
