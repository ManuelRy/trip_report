FutureAI Workplace Summit 2025

Exploring cutting-edge AI integration for Taskly's platform enhancement

September 22-24, 2025
Bangkok, Thailand
Technical Lead & Visionary Leader
$2,340 Total Cost

Conference Overview

This three-day business trip to Bangkok, Thailand, was for Headangelly Huy (Visionary Leader) and Sokleng Ry (Technical Lead) to attend the "FutureAI Workplace Summit 2025." This international conference focused on the latest advancements and applications of Artificial Intelligence in the workplace.

Key Objectives:

  • Gain cutting-edge knowledge on AI integration, particularly in intelligent scheduling and task prioritization
  • Network with global AI leaders and solution providers
  • Identify potential AI tools for enhancing Taskly's core product functionalities
  • Explore new avenues for innovation within our platform to further revolutionize productivity

Conference Impact & Next Steps

Attending the "FutureAI Workplace Summit 2025" in Bangkok was an extremely valuable and insightful experience for Headangelly Huy and Sokleng Ry. The conference provided a comprehensive understanding of the latest trends, ethical considerations, and practical applications of AI in diverse workplace settings, particularly in relation to productivity software.

Key Takeaways:

  • Advanced AI integration strategies for intelligent scheduling algorithms
  • Predictive analytics implementation for project management enhancement
  • Ethical AI frameworks for user trust and data security
  • Machine learning applications for task prioritization optimization

The knowledge gained from keynotes, workshops, and direct interactions with AI solution providers will directly inform Taskly's strategy for integrating advanced AI tools to boost efficiency, enhance our scheduling algorithms, and drive innovation across our platform. We are now well-equipped to propose specific AI initiatives and pilot projects based on the intelligence gathered, further solidifying Taskly's commitment to revolutionizing personal and professional productivity.
