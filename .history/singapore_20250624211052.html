Technical Lead & User Advocate

Research Conference Overview

This two-day business trip to Singapore was for Sokleng Ry (Technical Lead) and Sok Eng Huot (User Advocate) to attend "SocialPulse 2025: An International Business Research Presentation on Social Media Marketing." The event was held at The Sands Expo and Convention Centre, Marina Bay Sands.

Data Analytics

Latest research-backed insights into social media trends

Campaign Strategies

Effective digital marketing campaign methodologies

User Acquisition

Enhanced strategies for building online community

Platform Integration

Aligning sharing features with user behaviors

Conference Objectives: Acquire latest insights to refine and optimize Taskly's digital marketing efforts, enhance user acquisition, build stronger online community, and gather insights into user engagement with productivity tools on social platforms.

Marketing Strategy Enhancement & Implementation

Attending "SocialPulse 2025" in Singapore was an extremely valuable and enriching experience for Sokleng Ry and Sok Eng Huot. The in-depth research presentations and discussions provided cutting-edge insights into critical aspects of social media marketing, including algorithmic changes, ROI measurement for influencer campaigns, AI integration, and ethical considerations.

Strategic Implementation Plan

Immediate Actions:

  • Implement AI-driven content personalization for Taskly's social media channels
  • Develop comprehensive influencer partnership measurement framework
  • Enhanced user feedback collection through community engagement strategies
  • Update data privacy policies following latest regulatory guidelines

The knowledge gained will be instrumental in refining Taskly's social media strategies, allowing us to implement more effective, data-driven, and ethically sound campaigns. This will directly contribute to our user acquisition goals and enhance how we gather valuable feedback to continuously improve the Taskly experience, solidifying our mission to make scheduling effortless for everyone.

Expected Outcomes: Enhanced user engagement rates, improved conversion metrics, stronger brand community, and more effective social media ROI across all platforms.
