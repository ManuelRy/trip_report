Mondulkiri Feature Article Interview

Capturing Taskly's legacy through the insights of our esteemed retired founder

August 6-8, 2025
Sen Monorom, Mondulkiri
Product Architect
$620 Total Cost

Interview Mission

This three-day business trip to Mondulkiri, Cambodia, was undertaken by Vongsorya Uch, Taskly's Product Architect, to conduct an in-depth interview with Mr. Chan Vuthy, a highly respected retired staff member and founding developer of Taskly.

Interview Purpose:

  • Gather invaluable insights, experiences, and contributions during his long tenure with the company
  • Document Taskly's early product development challenges and architectural decisions
  • Capture the evolution of our user-centric design philosophy
  • Create content for a feature article in Taskly's official magazine
  • Preserve historical perspective on Taskly's journey and product vision
The material collected will be meticulously crafted into a feature article celebrating Mr. Vuthy's legacy and providing historical perspective on Taskly's journey, particularly concerning the evolution of its product vision and architecture.

Interview Journey

Day 1 - Wednesday, August 6, 2025

8:00 AM
Departure to Mondulkiri

Scenic drive from Phnom Penh via private car/SUV to Sen Monorom, Mondulkiri. Strategic stops for leg stretching and appreciating the changing landscape.

1:00 PM
Arrival & Preparation

Check-in at Phnong Retreat Resort, followed by final review of interview questions and recording equipment setup.

3:00 PM
First Interview Session with Mr. Chan Vuthy

Location: Mr. Chan Vuthy's home - No. 456, Keo Seima Village, Sen Monorom

Focus: Taskly's foundational years, initial product development challenges, rationale behind early architectural decisions, and journey from concept to first prototype.

Key Insight: Mr. Vuthy shared insights into the initial "pain points" Taskly aimed to solve for users and the challenges of building intuitive productivity tools.

7:00 PM
Initial Transcription

Dinner followed by initial transcription of interview notes and review of audio recordings, identifying key quotes related to Taskly's product evolution.

Day 2 - Thursday, August 7, 2025

10:00 AM
Second Interview Session with Mr. Chan Vuthy

Focus: Key product milestones, major feature releases, challenges in scaling Taskly's platform, and how user feedback shaped development over the decades.

Key Topics:

  • Maintaining intuitive user experience while adding complex functionalities
  • Perspectives on feature development and scalability challenges
  • Evolution of user feedback integration processes
  • Technical architecture decisions that shaped Taskly's growth
12:30 PM
Family Lunch

Lunch with Mr. Vuthy and his family, fostering a more personal connection and understanding of his life beyond Taskly.

2:00 PM
Content Organization

Preliminary outlining of the feature article, identifying key quotes, anecdotes about product breakthroughs, and narratives highlighting Taskly's commitment to user-centric design.

Day 3 - Friday, August 8, 2025

9:00 AM
Final Review

Final review of notes and audio recordings to ensure all necessary information for the feature article has been captured, particularly product-related historical data.

12:00 PM
Return Journey

Return journey from Sen Monorom, Mondulkiri, to Phnom Penh with comprehensive interview materials and historical insights.

Mission Accomplished

The three-day trip to Mondulkiri was highly successful in achieving its primary objective: securing a rich and insightful interview with Mr. Chan Vuthy, Taskly's esteemed retired staff member. Vongsorya Uch effectively captured his articulate recounting of Taskly's early product development, architectural challenges, and the evolution of its user-centric design.

This invaluable historical perspective will serve as the cornerstone of a compelling feature article for our company magazine, reinforcing Taskly's commitment to continuous innovation and user empowerment.

Next Steps:

  • Complete transcription of full interview recordings
  • Draft feature article highlighting key insights and historical moments
  • Editorial review and publication in Taskly's company magazine
  • Archive interview materials for future company history documentation

The Marketing team will now proceed with transcribing the full interview and drafting the article for publication, ensuring Mr. Vuthy's legacy and contributions to Taskly's success are properly documented and celebrated.
