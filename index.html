Overview

This two-day business trip to Sihanoukville, Cambodia, was undertaken by our CEO, Mr. Sovandara Phallim, and CFO, Ms. Headangelly Huy, to engage with two different potential investors. The primary objective was to present our company's robust expansion plans and compelling investment opportunities to secure strategic funding for our upcoming digital transformation initiatives.

Conclusion

The two-day business trip to Sihanoukville was highly productive and successfully met its primary objective of engaging with potential investors. Both meetings, with Coastal Horizons Group and Galaxy Capital Ventures, yielded positive outcomes, with strong indications of moving forward to the due diligence phase. The team effectively communicated our company's vision and presented compelling investment cases. We are optimistic about securing the necessary funding to fuel our strategic growth initiatives.
